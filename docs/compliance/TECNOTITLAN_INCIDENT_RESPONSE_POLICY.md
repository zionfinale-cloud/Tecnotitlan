# Tecnotitlan Incident Response Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-05

## Purpose

This policy defines Tecnotitlan's process for responding to suspected or confirmed security incidents involving systems, personal data, marketplace integrations, or payment workflows.

## Incident Examples

- Unauthorized access to administrative systems.
- Exposure of customer personal data.
- Loss or compromise of API keys, SMTP credentials, database credentials, or marketplace tokens.
- Suspicious order, payment, support, or integration activity.
- Malware or unauthorized software on an administrative device.

## Roles and Responsibilities

- Super Admin: coordinates incident response, disables affected access, rotates credentials, and communicates with providers.
- Operations/Admin staff: report suspicious activity immediately and preserve relevant details.
- External providers: hosting, database, payment, email, and marketplace providers may be contacted when their systems or credentials are involved.

## Response Process

1. Identify and document the issue.
2. Contain the incident by disabling affected accounts, tokens, integrations, or services when necessary.
3. Rotate exposed credentials and review logs.
4. Restore service safely.
5. Notify affected providers, sellers, customers, or authorities when required by law or contract.
6. Record lessons learned and update controls.

## Reporting Channel

Security or privacy issues may be reported to Tecnotitlan through the official support or administrative contact channels.
