# Tecnotitlan Personal Data Protection Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-05

## Purpose

Tecnotitlan protects personal data used for ecommerce operations, customer support, order fulfillment, payment processing, marketplace operations, and business administration.

## Personal Data Processed

Tecnotitlan may process customer name, email, phone number, shipping address, order details, support messages, payment status, tracking information, and marketplace order identifiers.

## Processing Purposes

- Create and manage customer accounts.
- Process orders and payments.
- Fulfill shipments and provide tracking updates.
- Provide customer support.
- Manage inventory, returns, refunds, and marketplace operations.
- Comply with tax, legal, and business obligations.

## Access and Confidentiality

Personal data access is limited to authorized staff with a business need. Administrative access is protected by authentication and role-based permissions.

## User Requests

Tecnotitlan will assist with valid requests to update, delete, or provide customer data when required by law, seller obligations, marketplace requirements, or platform policy.

## Retention and Deletion

Personal data will be retained only as needed for business operations, legal obligations, tax records, customer support, fraud prevention, and marketplace requirements. At the end of a contractual relationship, data no longer required will be deleted or anonymized where practical and legally permitted.

## Breach Notification

Tecnotitlan will review suspected data incidents and notify affected parties, sellers, service providers, TikTok Shop, or authorities when required by law or contract.
